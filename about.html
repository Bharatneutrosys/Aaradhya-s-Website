About Aaradhya

Local roots. International care. Travel that feels personal.

Founded in Kathmandu, Aaradhya Travels and Tours has grown around one simple belief: travellers deserve clear advice, honest guidance, and people who care after the booking is confirmed.

Our Story

Built on trust, refined through experience

Since 1998, Aaradhya has helped guests explore Nepal's mountains, cities, sacred places, and wildlife regions while also arranging international holidays with the same careful attention. We work with experienced guides, reliable transport teams, and accommodation partners who understand comfort, timing, and hospitality.

Our role is to make the journey feel considered: the right pace, the right hotel, the right guide, and the right backup when plans change.

Our mission

To design journeys that are inspiring, realistic, and responsibly handled, so every traveller feels informed before departure and cared for throughout the trip.
